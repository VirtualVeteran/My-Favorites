Oh the places She'll go...

Towns I have lived In

  • FLORIDA

    • I lived in Florida the longest, but in each town roughly about 4 years. We moved more than the list below, probably about 10 or more times while I was growing up. The denomination that my father was a part of believed in rotating their Reverands to prevent stagnation in congregations.

    • Sebring

      • This is the first church I remember, but there were others before. This was the first offical location my father had by himself. The congregation was very old, and they passed away frequently. I probably saw 15 open casket funerals from age 6-10, which I found very interesting at the time.

    • Cocoa

      • This was a much better time in my life, the church had a kid my age, and the kid had a ranch that I worked on from age 11-16. I got into 4 H and raised chickens/goats.

    • Port Charlotte

      • We moved here without a job, because my dad was worried about his mom's health. It was a very rough time financially, (because my father and mother had seven kids),but we did get to go to the beach more often.

  • GEORGIA

    • I moved to Georgia when my father left the ministry, and graduation in Warner Robins. After joining the USAF, I was stationed in Valdosta for 5 years.

    • Warner Robins

      • My dad was offically out of work and ministry, but found a job with a family friend in GA. We moved during my senior year, and I graduated with strangers. The silver lining was meeting my bestfriend and current spouse of almost 10 years. I got married at 19 in this town.

    • Valdosta

      • I moved here the day I married my spouse and worked odd jobs for 2 years while working on my Associates. I joined the USAF, and got stationed in Valdosta with my spouse who was already Active Duty USAF at that location.

    Towns I would like to Visit

    I would like to see the area around Seattle, and hopefully whale watch. In Kennebunkport, I would like to go collect clams and make authentic clam chowder. In Amsterdam, I would like to bike around the city with my spouse.
